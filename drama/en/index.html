FAQ

Questions creators keep asking

What is an AI short drama generator?
An AI short drama generator is a web tool that turns a written prompt, script or novel chapter into a vertical 30–120 second drama video. It combines large language models for scripting with video models such as Sora 2, Veo 3.1 and Kling 3 to produce scenes, voices, captions and sound — all inside the browser.
Is the QingxiHub AI short drama generator really free?
Yes. You can preview drafts without an account. Join the pilot when you want saved projects, batch planning or provider-specific rendering support.
Which AI video model is best for short drama in 2026?
Production teams don't pick one — they route by scene. Sora 2 is best for character consistency and VFX, Veo 3.1 for cinematic 4K and native audio, and Kling 3 Pro for physics-accurate human motion. QingxiHub lets you switch per scene in one project.
Can I use AI short dramas on TikTok, YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels?
Yes. All exports are 9:16 vertical MP4 with captions and platform-safe audio, optimised for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels and WeChat Channels. Episode length and hook placement follow each platform's retention best practice.
How is QingxiHub different from ReelShort or DramaBox?
ReelShort and DramaBox are consumer apps — you watch content there. QingxiHub is a creator tool — you generate episodes here, then distribute them to ReelShort, DramaBox, PineDrama, MyDrama, TikTok, YouTube Shorts or your own site.
Can I monetise AI short dramas I generate here?
Yes, but confirm the rights from the model provider and any music or asset source you use before publishing. Most creators monetize through platform programs, affiliate links, sponsorships, direct licensing or their own audience funnel.
What languages are supported?
Scripting, voiceover and captions work in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi, Bahasa Indonesia, Arabic, Japanese, Korean and Mandarin. We support one-click translation between any two.
Do I need to know prompt engineering?
No. Every template ships with pre-tuned prompt chains. You only fill in character name, setting and hook — we handle cinematography, pacing, music and captions.
Do I need my own API key to use QingxiHub?
No. The normal flow is hosted: sign in, choose a template, generate and export. Connecting your own provider is only for advanced users who want more control over routing or spend.
Can I switch to my own provider account later?
Yes. Start simple first, then connect your own provider later if you want tighter billing control or a custom workflow. That option should feel like an upgrade, not a requirement.
Is AI-generated drama content legal?
Yes, as long as you own or have rights to the source material. We disclose AI-generation in exports and follow platform policies (YouTube, TikTok, Meta). For commercial work we recommend using original scripts or licensed IP.
What export formats are supported?
9:16 MP4 (default), 1:1 MP4, and 16:9 MP4 for YouTube. 1080p on free plans, up to 4K on the Studio plan. Optional captions as SRT / VTT and a separate voiceover WAV for custom edits.
