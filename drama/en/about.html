About QingxiHub

QingxiHub is an independent AI short drama studio and knowledge hub. We route each shot across Sora 2, Veo 3.1 and Kling 3 Pro — the three best frontier video models for vertical drama — and publish honest guides so creators can produce, distribute and monetize their own IP.

What we do

  • Multi-model routing: Our generator picks the right model for each shot — Veo for establishing, Kling for dialogue, Sora for reaction and transformation.
  • Bring-your-own-key (BYOK): You pay the model provider directly. No markup, no per-second tax.
  • Editorial guides: Independent reviews of tools, benchmarks of models, and playbooks for genre templates (werewolf, CEO romance, revenge) that dominate the category.
  • Creator-first economics: We help creators self-distribute to TikTok, YouTube Shorts and DramaBox — not lock them into a platform that takes 80% of revenue.

What we believe

Creators should own their IP. Licensing 100% of your rights for a 15% revenue share was a 2022 deal. In 2026, you can produce, publish and keep full ownership.
No single model wins every shot. Sora 2 nails reaction shots. Veo 3.1 wins on establishing. Kling 3 is the dialogue and cliffhanger king. A multi-model router beats any single-model tool.
Transparency beats charisma. Every page discloses affiliate relationships. Every benchmark shows its methodology. Every ranking shows what we tested.
Free should mean free. Our UI is free. We pay our bills through (a) affiliate commissions when readers sign up with model providers through our links, (b) Google AdSense on articles. Neither raises your cost.

Editorial standards

  • We test every tool we review for at least one full 60-second vertical drama before ranking it.
  • We disclose affiliate relationships on every page where they appear.
  • We update rankings quarterly and mark each article with the last-updated date.
  • We do not accept payment to change a ranking. Sponsors get labeled posts, period.
  • We correct errors publicly. If we're wrong, we say so in the article.
