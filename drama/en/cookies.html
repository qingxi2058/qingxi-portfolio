Last updated: April 24, 2026. A short, honest explanation of what cookies run on QingxiHub and how to control them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, measure how a site is used, and (sometimes) to personalize advertising.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies come from third-party services we use:

  • Google AdSense / DoubleClick: See Google's advertising policy.
  • Google Analytics: See Google's privacy policy.

How to control cookies

  • Browser settings: Every major browser lets you block or delete cookies. Blocking all cookies will break essential features of QingxiHub.
  • Google Ads personalization: Opt out at Google Ads Settings.
  • Industry opt-out: aboutads.info (US) · youronlinechoices.com (EU).
  • Do Not Track: We honor the DNT header where technically feasible.
