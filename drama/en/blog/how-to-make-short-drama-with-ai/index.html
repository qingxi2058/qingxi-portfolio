Workflow guide: make a 60-second AI short drama from one prompt

Start with a clean hook, shape it into three beats, then route each scene to the model that fits the job instead of guessing.

12-step simplified workflow

  1. Choose one hook, not three competing ideas.
  2. Write a 3-beat outline before touching models.
  3. Route dialogue-heavy scenes to consistency-first tools and motion-heavy scenes to animation-first tools.
  4. Export once for preview, then polish captions and audio after retention checks.
